Ingredients

You will almost certainly have to pay a visit to your local oriental market if you don't already have these ingredients.
Notice: More precise amounts will be posted after the first class on May 31. Hee hee.
Ingredient Amount Notes
Vegetable / Canola Oil A Lot DO NOT use oils that have an inherent taste, such as olive oil.
Alcohol A Lot Use beer or white wine since cooking alcohol is expensive. Don't worry. Expect no loss or mutation in flavor.
Dried Chili Pepper Flakes
辣椒面 (là jiāo miàn) 		A Lot
Dried Chili Pepper Powder
辣椒粉 (là jiāo fěn) 		Some May be difficult to acquire in the US. You can make your own by grinding dried chili pepper flakes into powder. The most convenient way I can think of is using a blender. Or, you can replace this ingredient with more dried chili pepper flakes.
Sesame Seeds Some
Salt A Bit
Peppercorn
花椒 (huā jiāo) 		A Few
Star Anise
八角 (bā jiǎo) 		1 or 2
